About Mission

A dog with great vibes and even better manners.

Mission was discovered during our tour of the Spanish Missions of San Antonio. He was found in poor health, crumpled in a ditch unmoving. We got him to animal control for treatment, and he has since made a full recovery. However, due to limited space at the shelter, we were forced to adopt him to avoid capacity euthansia.

Mission is a friendly pup, estimated to be 2-3 years old by the vet. While he appears to be house-trained, he doesn't seem to know any words. He loves to cuddle but has a hard time figuring out how to lay down without face planting. He is very soft and submissive around our own two dogs and cat. He is bit reactive to cars and tall people, but we are currently working on that.

Right now he's sharing a camper with us, and while he loves his crew, he's ready for a space to truly call his own.
